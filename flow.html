Applicant Journey Flow

NHA application flow — 2023 to 2025. Use the Year, School, and Grade dropdowns to filter.

How to use: Click a journey bar in Top Ranked Applicant Outcomes to highlight that path across all panels. The flow diagram dims unused routes and the outcome bars update. Hover any node or edge for counts. Double-click to deselect.
