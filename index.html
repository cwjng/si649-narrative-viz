Understanding the
Applicant Journey
at NHA

This project narrates how prospective students move through National Heritage Academies' enrollment process — from initial application through first days in the classroom. Rather than just telling, these visualizations show the full complexity of applicant paths.

Visualize the many possible paths an applicant can take through NHA’s enrollment process — because the complexity is better shown than described.
Explore and characterize the types of applicants appearing in NHA’s data from the 2023–2025 academic years to surface patterns and archetypes.
Support NHA’s understanding of where applicants are lost, committed, or redirected throughout the enrollment funnel.
Data Access Statement Data supporting this analysis cannot be made available due to client confidentiality and NDA restrictions. Code and methodological details are provided to enable replication with comparable data.
